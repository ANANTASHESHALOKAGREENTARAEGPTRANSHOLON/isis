= Fundamentals
include::_attributes.adoc[]

This guide introduces the xref:ug:fun:core-concepts.adoc[core concepts] and ideas behind Apache Isis,
and tells you how to xref:ug:fun:getting-started/helloworld-archetype.adoc[get started] with a Maven archetype.

It also describes a number of xref:ug:fun:how-tos.adoc[how-to]s, describes how to influence the
xref:ug:vw:layout.adoc[UI layout] of your domain objects (this is ultimately just a type of metadata), and it
 catalogues various xref:support:dg:dg.adoc#_dg_hints-and-tips.adoc[FAQ]s.
